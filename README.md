## 🚀 About Me

Servas Adolph is a PhD student in the Department of Future Convergence Technology at **Soonchunhyang University, South Korea**. He received his Master's degree in Big Data Engineering from Soonchunhyang University in 2023, and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and Information Technology from the **United African University of Tanzania**.

His research interests primarily focus on **multimodal machine learning for healthcare**, health data analytics, and artificial intelligence. With a strong academic background and experience in software engineering, Servas is dedicated to advancing technology and implementing innovative solutions in healthcare and related domains.

🌍 From Tanzania &nbsp;•&nbsp; 📍 Based in South Korea

## 🔭 What I'm Currently Working On

- **WBC AI Model:** Improving model performance with advanced training techniques for white blood cell detection and classification.
- **Medical AI Report Generator:** Developing a system to produce doctor-like patient comments and clinical reports.
- **Generative Models:** Exploring generative AI for medical imaging and healthcare applications.

## 🌱 Currently Learning

- Advanced generative model architectures for medical imaging (diffusion models, GANs).
- Large language model fine-tuning for clinical text generation.
